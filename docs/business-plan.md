# 📊 GeoTarget Pro — Business Plan

## Executive Summary

**GeoTarget Pro** is a SaaS geofencing platform that empowers businesses of all sizes to create virtual geographic boundaries, trigger real-time actions when users enter/exit those boundaries, and gain deep audience analytics — all while maintaining full GDPR/CCPA compliance.

**Mission:** Democratize location-based marketing by making powerful geofencing tools accessible and affordable for small to enterprise-level businesses.

**Vision:** Become the go-to geofencing platform for 100,000+ businesses worldwide by 2029.

## 2. Revenue Model

### 2.1 Primary Revenue: SaaS Subscription

Monthly/Annual subscription with tiered pricing based on features and usage.

### 2.2 Secondary Revenue: CPM (Cost Per Thousand Impressions)

Charge per 1,000 notification impressions delivered within geofences.

### 2.3 Additional Revenue Streams

- **API Access Fees** — Per-call pricing for enterprise integrations
- **Data Insights Reports** — Premium anonymized market intelligence reports
- **White-Label Licensing** — For agencies and resellers
- **Onboarding & Training** — Professional services
